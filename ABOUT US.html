CEO of Klinik Rasa

I'm Julia Child, and I'm very proud to introduce our company to you. At Klinik Rasa, we believe that food is more than a necessity; it is an experience that connects us to our culture, traditions, and each other. 

We are committed to providing food products that are not only delicious, but also healthy and high quality. By prioritizing natural ingredients and recipes inspired by Indonesia's culinary heritage, we strive to provide the best for you and your family.
 
We want every bite to bring happiness and warmth to your dining table. Thank you for joining us on this journey. Let's create special moments together!

Our History

2006 

When the shop was first established. The first product sold was a vegetable salad, which is now a signature of Klinik Rasa.

2007-2010

Klinik Rasa is growing and innovating to sell a variety of other healthy food menus.

2011

The growing number of customers of Klinik Rasa led to the opening of a second store in Jakarta.

2012-Now

Klinik Rasa is growing and has many loyal customers spread across JABODETABEK.

Vision & Mission

Vision

To be a leading healthy food store recognized nationally and internationally, providing high-quality and innovative products that support healthy lifestyles, and committed to improving public health. We strive to create an educational and inspiring shopping environment, where customers can find a wide selection of nutritious, organic and eco-friendly food.

Mission

1. Product Quality: Offering a wide selection of healthy foods that are guaranteed quality and safety. 

2. Consumer Education: Increase public awareness about the importance of healthy eating through information and education programs. 

3.  Diversity of Choice: Providing a wide range of products, including organic, gluten-free, and vegan-friendly foods, to meet various customer needs. 

4. Community Support: Collaborate with local farmers and food producers to support the local economy and sustainability. 

5. Customer Service: Provide friendly and professional service, ensuring a pleasant shopping experience.
